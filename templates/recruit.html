📋 Recruit Licensed Adjusters

Experienced flood adjusters can join your team immediately by verifying their license. No extra training required — they already know the business.

How it works

  • 1 You send an invitation link or they apply via the recruitment page
  • 2 They provide their state adjuster license number and select their state
  • 3 System auto-verifies the license against state databases where available
  • 4 If verified → they're automatically added to your team as an Adjuster
  • 5 If verification is pending → you manually approve after reviewing
✅ Licensed Adjuster Benefits
• Instant access to assigned claims
• Can create and edit claims immediately
• Full access to Aquila AI assistant
• No training module required

🔧 Recruit Contractors → Adjusters

Contractors who want to become flood adjusters go through a structured onboarding pipeline: license verification, training modules, and certification testing.

Onboarding Pipeline

  • 1 Contractor applies with their current contractor license & experience
  • 2 You review their background and approve them into training
  • 3 They complete training modules:
        • Flood damage assessment fundamentals
        • Water categories & classification
        • NFIP & FEMA guidelines
        • Xactimate / estimating basics
        • FloodClaims Pro platform training
  • 4 They take the certification test (must score 80%+)
  • 5 Upon passing → you activate them as a licensed adjuster on your team
📘 Training & Licensing Notes
• Contractor license is verified but does not substitute for an adjuster license
• Each state has different adjuster licensing requirements
• Training modules are self-paced — typically 2-4 weeks
• Certification test can be retaken after 7 days
• Until certified, they have read-only access to claims
