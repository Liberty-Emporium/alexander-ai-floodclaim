Send automatic SMS updates to clients when their claim status changes (e.g., "Your claim FC-2026-001 has been updated to: In Progress").
📖 How to set up Twilio:
  1. Go to twilio.com and create a free account (trial gives you ~$15 credit)
  2. Verify your personal phone number (for trial testing)
  3. Buy a phone number: Dashboard → Phone Numbers → Buy a Number (~$1/month)
  4. Copy your Account SID (starts with AC...) and Auth Token from the dashboard
  5. Paste them below with your Twilio phone number
  6. SendGrid (email) and Twilio (SMS) can use the same account at twilio.com

Get a weekly email summary with: new claims, closed claims, total open claims, and pipeline value. Requires SendGrid API key — go to section 4 (📧 SendGrid Email) above and save your SENDGRID_API_KEY first.
📖 How It All Works

🤖 AI Photo Analysis — When you upload a damage photo on a claim, FloodClaims Pro sends it to your selected AI model via OpenRouter. The AI describes what it sees (water damage levels, affected materials, repair needs) and saves the description on the claim report.

🌊 Aquila Chat — The chat bubble (bottom-right corner) is your AI assistant. Aquila can create claims, add rooms and line items, move claims through the pipeline, schedule inspections, look up FEMA flood zones, check NFIP compliance, send client notifications, and more.

🧠 Train Aquila — Use the brain editor above to customize Aquila's identity, personality, memory, and system instructions. Changes take effect immediately.

🔑 All powered by: OPENROUTER_API_KEY environment variable on Railway. Get a key at openrouter.ai.
