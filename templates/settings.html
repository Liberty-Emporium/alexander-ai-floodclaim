📖 How AI Analysis Works

When you upload a damage photo on a claim, FloodClaim Pro sends it to your selected model via OpenRouter for instant damage assessment.

The AI describes what it sees — water damage levels, affected materials, likely repair needs — and saves the description alongside the photo on the claim report.
